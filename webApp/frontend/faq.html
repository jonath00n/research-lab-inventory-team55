Frequently Asked Questions

What is LabRat?

LabRat is your organizational tool for lab management at Texas A&M University! Powered by machine learning, your experience can be streamlined for simple and effective use! From this website, users are able to checkout items and those in higher positions are able to manage and observe activity in the lab.

How do I use LabRat?

First, access the user registration page by clicking on the user icon on the top right of the home page. From there, enter your information to register your account and you can sign-in whenever you want! To check items back into the lab, we require the use of our mobile Android app to scan items for return.

How do I change my classification from "Student" to "Profesor" or "Admin"?

Contact a current admin of LabRat. Get a reference from a professor or person that you know to be an admin, or contact LabRat services here: labratservices.ecen@gmail.com.

How do I change my password for my account?

Currently, there is no functionality to reset passwords due to complications with authentication tokens. If you find yourself forgetting your password, please contact labratservices.ecen@gmail.com and we will disable your previous account.
